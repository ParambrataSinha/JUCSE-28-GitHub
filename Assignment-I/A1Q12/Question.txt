The length and breadth of a rectangle are input through the keyboard. Write a program to determine  [OPTIONAL]
(i) radius of a circle and ratio of perimeters of the rectangle and the circle if the areas of the rectangle and the circle are equal 
(ii) radius of the circle and the ratio of the areas of the rectangle and the circle if the perimeters of the rectangle and the circle are equal.

Solution Methods:
    1. Using Simple I/O and Arithmetic Operations
    2. Using multiple Functions
    3. Using Structures
